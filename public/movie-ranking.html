The Best Movies According to Christine

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Empire Records

A cult classic showcasing 90's American teenage culture with an amazing soundtrack.

The Boy and the Heron

The newest anime from Miyazaki and Studio Ghibli; an abstract story of the cycle of life through the lense of Eastern philosophy.

Flash Gordon

A timeless space opera superhero film with eclectic characters, based on a intergalactic adventure from comic strip in the 1930's.
